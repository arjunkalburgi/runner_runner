RUNNERRUNNER: HOW TO PLAY

Step One: Export all contents of the zip file into the MATLAB directory. 
Step Two: Open Runner_Runner.m file in MATLAB. 
Step Three: Press F5.
Step Four: Play RUNNERRUNNER! Follow the instructions and have fun.
